Account Deletion

ASV Skopje - How to Delete Your Account and Data

🔒 Account and Data Deletion Request

At ASV Skopje, we respect your privacy and your right to control your personal data. If you wish to permanently delete your account and associated data, we've made the process clear and straightforward.

⚠️ Important: Account deletion is permanent and cannot be reversed. Once your account is deleted, all associated data will be removed from our systems within 30 days.

How to Request Account Deletion

You can request to delete your account through one of the following methods:

Option 1: In-App Deletion (Recommended)

  1. Open the ASV Skopje app
  2. Navigate to Settings → Account
  3. Scroll down and select Delete Account
  4. Read the warning and confirm by entering your password
  5. Your request will be processed immediately

Option 2: Email Request

Send an email to our support team with the following information:

📧 Support Contact

Email: support@asv.mk

Subject Line: "Account Deletion Request"

Include in your email:

  • Your registered email address or username
  • Your full name (as registered in the app)
  • A statement: "I request permanent deletion of my account and all associated data"

What Data Gets Deleted

When you request account deletion, the following personal data is permanently removed:

  • Account Information: Email address, username, password, profile name
  • User Profile Data: All personal profile information and preferences
  • Activity Records: Login history, activity logs, and timestamps
  • Associated Cases: References to cases you were associated with (soft delete)
  • Push Notification Tokens: Device registration for notifications
  • Device Settings: App preferences and configuration data

What Data Is Retained

For legal, audit, and system integrity purposes, certain information may be retained for a limited time:

  • Legal Records: Case documents and judicial records (as required by law)
  • Audit Logs: Anonymized system logs for security (30 days)
  • Transaction Records: Records required for compliance (if applicable)

These retained records cannot be used to identify you and are kept only for legal compliance and system security.

Deletion Timeline

Day 1
Immediate Deactivation
Your account is immediately locked and inaccessible. You cannot log in or access any services.
Days 2-7
Cancellation Period
During this grace period, you can contact support to cancel the deletion request (email support@asv.mk).
Days 8-30
Data Deletion
Personal data is permanently deleted from our primary systems and backups.
Day 30+
Deletion Complete
Your account and personal data have been completely removed from our systems.

Important Considerations

⚠️ Before Deleting Your Account:
  • Download or export any important case files or documents you need (case documents will be retained per law)
  • Inform any colleagues who rely on your expertise and case assignments
  • Ensure you have alternative means of case communication
  • This action cannot be undone

Canceling Your Deletion Request

You have 7 days after submitting your deletion request to cancel it. To cancel, contact our support team immediately:

📧 Email: support@asv.mk

Subject: "Cancel Account Deletion Request"

Include your email address or username and a clear statement that you wish to cancel the deletion.

  • GDPR (General Data Protection Regulation) for EU users
  • Local data protection regulations in Macedonia
  • International privacy standards and best practices
